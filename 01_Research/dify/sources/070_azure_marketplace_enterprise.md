# Dify Enterprise Now Available in the Microsoft Azure Marketplace

Published: Jul 30, 2025

Dify today announced the availability of Dify Enterprise in the Microsoft Azure Marketplace, an online store providing applications and services for use on Azure. Dify customers can now take advantage of the productive and trusted Azure cloud platform, with streamlined deployment and management. Azure customers worldwide can now enjoy streamlined purchasing and billing processes for Dify Enterprise, eliminating the complexity of procurement process.

## Enterprise Features

Dify Enterprise offers tools and LLMs that enterprises need to build, deploy, and scale AI applications directly within their Azure infrastructure, while maintaining existing compliance frameworks and security standards.

Dify Enterprise offers an all-in-one platform for enterprises to build, deploy and manage AI application at scale, including:
- Agentic workflow
- RAG pipeline
- Extensive integrations

## Deployment Modes

After placing an order on Azure Marketplace, customers may choose their preferred deployment mode:
- **On-premise deployment** — for maximum compliance and data control
- **Cloud deployment** — for fast, scalable enterprise AI activation

## Enterprise-Grade Features

Dify Enterprise includes enterprise-grade features including:
- **Advanced multi-tenant management**
- **Seamless SSO integration** (SAML, OIDC, OAuth2)
- **Centralized access control**
- **Comprehensive audit logging**

Enterprise customers also benefit from:
- Priority support channels
- Professional services options
- Negotiated service-level agreements (SLAs)
