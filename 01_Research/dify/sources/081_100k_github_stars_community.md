# 100K Stars on GitHub: Thank You to Our Amazing Open Source Community

Published: June 5, 2025

## Dify's Open Source Growth

Two years ago, on May 15, 2023, Dify was open-sourced with a simple goal: to explore how developers could more easily build applications powered by large language models. Today, Dify has surpassed 100,000 stars on GitHub, placing it among the top 100 open source projects globally.
